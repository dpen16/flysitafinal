Product Policy for Vendors

All sellers are expected to adhere to the following policies when listing products on Genuine Garments. Seller offenses and prohibited content can result in suspension of your Genuine Garments account.This policy requires that sellers act fairly and honestly on Genuine Garments to ensure a safe buying and selling experience. All sellers must:

  • Provide accurate information to company and our customers at all times
  • Not send unsolicited or inappropriate communications
  • Not attempt to circumvent the company's sales process
  • Not operate more than one selling account on company without a legitimate business need
  • Act fairly and not misuse company’s features or services

Accurate Information

You must provide accurate information to Company and our customers, and update the information if it changes. For example, this means that you must use a business name that accurately identifies your business and list your products in the correct category.

Acting Fairly

You must act fairly and lawfully and may not misuse any service provided by Company.Examples of unfair activities include:

  • Providing misleading or inappropriate information to Company or our customers, such as by creating multiple detail pages for the same product or posting offensive product images
  • Manipulating sales rank (such as by accepting fake orders or orders that you have paid for) or making claims about sales rank in product titles or descriptions
  • Attempting to increase the price of a product after an order is confirmed
  • Artificially inflating web traffic (using bots or paying for clicks, for example)
